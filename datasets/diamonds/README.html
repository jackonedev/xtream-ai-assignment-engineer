Diamonds Dataset

Key features include the 4 Cs: carat, cut, color, and clarity, along with physical measurements such as depth, table, price, x, y, and z.


The Carat is a unit of mass, equal to 200 mg, used for measuring gemstones and pearls. The Cut Grade is an objective assessment of a diamond's light performance, often perceived as sparkle.
